Tests

Exam Prep — At a Glance

Know which exam you need, the target score, and how long to prepare.

📘

IELTS

English proficiency for UK, AUS, CA, NZ.

  • Target: 6.5 – 7.5
  • Fee: ~$245
  • Prep: 2–3 months
📗

TOEFL

English proficiency widely accepted in USA.

  • Target: 90 – 110
  • Fee: ~$220
  • Prep: 2–3 months
📕

GRE

For MS programs in USA & many other countries.

  • Target: 310 – 325+
  • Fee: ~$220
  • Prep: 3–4 months
📒

GMAT

For MBA & business analytics programs.

  • Target: 650 – 720+
  • Fee: ~$275
  • Prep: 3–5 months
📓

PTE

Fast results, accepted in UK, AUS, CA, NZ.

  • Target: 65 – 79
  • Fee: ~$210
  • Prep: 1–2 months
📔

Duolingo

Affordable, fully online English test.

  • Target: 110 – 130
  • Fee: ~$59
  • Prep: 2–4 weeks
